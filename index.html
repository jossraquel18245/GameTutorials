Arcade

Artist statement: I always find that games are good destressors for me. Especially now with the Pandemic where we can't go out as much I can play games with my friends online. I wanted to create this game because eventhough it's not as complex as other games it's a fun little way to get entertained while being at home.

The future goal with this page is to create other mini games within the page to have it be like a little Arcade. I think this would be fun to explore and have other types of games in.
